About Us

Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company. Our hotels are a haven of comfort and accessibility that reflect the Church culture and are designed to support a temple oriented stay.

Temple Spotlight

The Salt Lake Temple, in Salt Lake City, Utah, is the best-known temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It stands as the centerpiece of Temple Square, a 10-acre attraction that draws millions of visitors a year.
